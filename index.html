SUMMARY
Hello. I'm Subhadip Mitra. 

At the core I am an avid technologist who has his interests in Finance and IT. This current 
webpage is here to serve only as an online footprint of mine and code repository, beyond my 
professional affiliations amidst other things.

I'm a Linux fanatic with interests in kernels, algorithms, databases, system administration, 
application development and novice technologies. In the past I have enjoyed creating 
scalable and highly distributed systems for enterprises as well as connecting people with 
technology solutions that are easy to use, affordable, and sustainable over time.

These days as side projects, I am working with Python + Keras based computer vision for a 
more engaging social networking experience with realtime quantifiable human emotions. One 
of my other projects, include Black-Scholes/Merton market and Bayesian price convergence 
for arbitrage free option pricing.

When mathematics and coding are not keeping me up through the lean weekends, I find myself 
fascinated by the Astrophysics. I try to keep up with the latest happenings in theoretical 
physics and hence herein you would find me sharing some quirky ideas in the same, hopefully 
with some fancy yet nifty mathematical proof to go along with.
